      - Generate APIS for types, and brands (get all)
      - Create paginations and special params

4 - In Discount: working with apply discount for products with grpc calls and proto services (get, post, put, delete).
5 - In Ordering: working with created orders (get by username, update, delete, checkout or order).

7 - Connect between basket and discount to check if products in basket has discount and calculate based on discount value
8 - Connect between catalog and discount to get each product discount if has and calculate
9 - Create events with RabbitMQ and Masstransit between basket and ordering services to handle checkout event to create new order
